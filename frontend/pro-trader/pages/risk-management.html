Risk Recommendations

  • Limit risk to 1–2% of capital per trade to protect long-term equity.
  • Aim for a minimum RRR of 1:2 — potential reward should be at least twice the risk.
  • Avoid having more than 5 open positions simultaneously to reduce correlated drawdown.
  • Pause trading if max drawdown exceeds 10% of capital in any single week.
